Company Statement

Columna Soft activates and acts as service provider in both software and hardware areas of Information Technology.

Columna Soft can offer software services ranging from software architecture and design, data modeling to software development, solution implementation, technical writing and tests plans development (for system verification testing, functional verification testing, installation verification testing and other types of tests)

In order to be able to offer a complete solution that combines software and hardware products, customized for customer needs, or maybe only high quality hardware products and other IT complementary solutions, we have established partnerships with well-known and well respected companies such as DELL, Lexmark and Symantec.

Through its business partners, Columna Soft can offer a wide selection of products in hardware realm, from compact laptops to printers, servers, storage and networking solutions. Other IT complementary solutions that can be offered include anti-virus, security solution, identity and information protection solution, backup and cloud-based solutions.
